Travel Tracker

The world has changed a lot since the pandemic, in particular, travel.
There is now far more information you need to be aware of and carry with you while moving around and technology is yet to catch up with this.

There is currently a gap in the market for something to help with this new problem that I was looking to solve.

Research

Competitor Analysis

SWOT analysis was carried out on 3 direct, and 2 indirect competitors.

User Interviews

A total of 5 interviews were carried out in a semi-strutctured format, each with 10 questions and lasted around 10 minutes.

User Persona

From the information gathered during the interviews, a user persona was generated to be used going forward in the design process.

Affinity Diagram

Interview responses were sorted, grouped and categorised to produce an affinity diagram that could be used during iteration.

Empathy Map

An empathy map was prodcued to gather examples of what users may be thinking, doing, saying or feeling during their current trip planning process.

Feature Prioritisation Matrix

Features mentioned during the interview process were added to a prioritisation matrix to show how users would value each feature compared to how difficult they would be to impliment

The Problem

During our interviews, we discovered that some of the biggest frustrations are having to look at multiple sources when booking a holiday and the ways you have transport your plans/itinerary with you while away.

We believe that finding a way to search for parts of your holiday and create an itinerary in one app will reduce the stress to the user both when planning the holiday and actually experiencing it.

We might do this by having the app help you search multiple places and automatically import bookings into an itinerary you can store on your phone so you easily access it while travelling. This will make the planning process much easier and might increase the frequency at which they travel.

The statement above is the problem being taken forward to solve in the ideation stage of the project.

Iteration

User Flow

I made a user flow to give the process the user would go through when using the app to decide which parts needed making for the prototype.

Paper Prototype

Initial prototypes were sketched out on paper to get a starting point quickly that could then be built from digitally.

Testing Round 1

Guerrilla user testing was carried out on the initial prototype to give some starting improvemnts to make while converting to a digital prototype.

Lo-Fi Prototype

These changes were made as the prototype became a lo-fi version to test further.

Testing Round 2

The lo-fi prototype was put through another round of guerrilla testing and a number of changes to be mare were generated.

Iterate

The changes were implimented and the final hi-fi prototype was created having receieved a total of 2 rounds of testing during the process.
